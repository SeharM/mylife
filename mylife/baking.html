Desserts which I have made so far.

Oreo Balls

Oreo balls are a wonderful dessert to have. It is also very simple to make. No baking necessary. All you do is take one pack of double stuffed oreos and make them all crumbly. Then you take one pack of Philidelphia cream cheese, and combine it all together. You make little tiny balls with the mixture you have created, and put them in the freezer for about 15-20 minutes. After that, you take the candy melts that you have which you can getr from Micheals and melt them. You take the oreo balls and put them in the candy melts and take them out once they are fully covered and put them in the fridge to cool. Whenever you want to take it out, it will be ready for you.

Red Velvet Cake

Red velvet cupcakes are amazing. It is a little hard for making it if you never made them, before because you want o get the correct ingredients. First you have to beat 1 1/2 cups of sugar until it is very light and fluffy and add in 2 eggs. Take 2 tablespoons of cocoa and mix it in with the red food color. Once you mix it add it to the eggs and sugar. Mix 1 teaspoon of salt with one teaspoon of vanilla and add 1 cup of butter milk. Add the 2 1/2 cups of flour which should be sifted and mix it all together. After mixing add in the 1 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda and 1 tablespoon of vinegar, and gently fold in it into the batter. Now you're batter is done, and pour it in to the pan. Youre oven should be pre-heated to 350 degrees F. Once you put it in, waut 30 minutes. Now time for the icing. Cook 5 tablespoons of flour and take 1 cup of milk and cook it till it is thick. Let it cool now. Now beat the 1 cup of sugar, 1 cup of butter room temp, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla. Mix till it is fluffy. Add the cooled flour into the mix, and mix it till it can soread easily. Now frost your cake
